ABOUT US

Start Point Education's founder has decided to start this business as they have a passion for the imporatance of learning to read. The founder's passion for reading will ensure the success for all children who attend.


Learning to read has always been, and is, a vital process of education.
The founder has begun this venture to provide children with fun and successful learning opportunities preparing them for school, or as additional support to their reading journey.


The founder is a highly qualified primary school teacher with nine years experience ranging from EYFS to Key Stage Two. They are currently undertaking their Educational Doctorate, with a focus on early reading, and how this could be improved using digital tools.
